Privacy Policy

Extension: Font Inspector Pro

Developer: ponITech

Last Updated: January 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ponITech handles information when you use the Font Inspector Pro browser extension.

Summary: Font Inspector Pro does not collect, store, or transmit any user data. Everything runs locally in your browser.

1. Information Collection

Font Inspector Pro is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect:

  • Personal information
  • Browsing history
  • Website content
  • Usage analytics
  • Any other user data

All font inspection and analysis occurs entirely within your local browser environment.

2. Permissions Explained

The extension requires minimal permissions to function:

  • activeTab: Allows the extension to inspect fonts only on the page you are currently viewing, and only when you activate the extension.
  • scripting: Enables the extension to read CSS properties (font-family, font-size, etc.) from page elements.

These permissions do not grant access to your browsing history, bookmarks, or any other browser data.

3. Third-Party Services

Font Inspector Pro does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, or external servers. No data is ever transmitted outside your browser.

4. Data Storage

The extension does not store any data persistently. Font information displayed during inspection exists only temporarily in memory and is discarded when you close the inspector or navigate away from the page.

5. Data Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to your information through this extension.

6. Children's Privacy

Font Inspector Pro does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
